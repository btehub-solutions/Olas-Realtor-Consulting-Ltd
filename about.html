About Olas Realtor Consulting Ltd

Building Trust, Delivering Excellence in Nigerian Real Estate

Who We Are

Olas Realtor Consulting Ltd is a premier real estate consulting firm headquartered in Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. Founded with a vision to transform the Nigerian real estate landscape, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in property sales, management, and professional training across Abeokuta and its surrounding areas.

Our comprehensive suite of services includes property sales, lettings, property management, and specialized training programs for aspiring real estate professionals. We pride ourselves on our commitment to transparency, professionalism, and delivering exceptional value to our clients.

With a team of seasoned professionals and a deep understanding of the Nigerian property market, we provide tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of each client, whether they are first-time buyers, seasoned investors, or property owners seeking professional management services.
